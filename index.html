Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge?
A global hackathon powered by NASA where participants solve real-world problems using space data—this year's challenge focuses on AI-based exoplanet discovery.
When and where is the challenge held?
The event is scheduled for 4th Oct untill 5th, and can be joined virtually or in-person at approved Space Apps locations worldwide.
How are we participating as Team Aramana?
We are engaging in the challenge titled “A World Away: Hunting for Exoplanets with AI”, building an intelligent model to explore publicly available NASA datasets.
What’s the goal of our project?
To develop a machine learning model with a user-friendly web interface capable of identifying confirmed exoplanets, candidates, and false positives.
What datasets will you use?
We’ll utilize open datasets from NASA's science archives.
Will your project be open-source?
Yes! Following NASA’s open-data ethos, we aim to make both our model and platform available to the public and researchers.
